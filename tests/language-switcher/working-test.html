About Vestigium

Vestigium (lat. footprint) was born in 2011 by the initiative of six women who listened to the needs of their neighbors in the Vrbana area, Zagreb.

As part of the Global Transition Movement for Sustainable Communities, we work to solve problems such as the growing distance between people.
